Synopsis

The problem our group came up with is the disorganized nature of having multiple Hybrid Synchronous Classes. In a Hybrid Synchronous format, this would amount to 4 separate zoom links and 4 different scheduled cohort times if a student took 4 classes. This is an issue for both instructor and students. Students often find themselves wasting time hunting for zoom links or finding their designated cohort assignments through hundreds of emails or through ELC. The confusing issue of cohorts is further exacerbated if the class rotates it's schedule of designated times for groups of students to come. This indirectly affects an Instructor's ability to teach if students are facing obstacles in joining class online and in-person.
