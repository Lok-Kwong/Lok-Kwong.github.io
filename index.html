Team Members

Lok Kwong, Vrund Parikh, Andrew Guest, and Alex Nguyen

Synopsis

For our term project, our group tackled a problem based on the disorganized nature of having multiple Hybrid Synchronous Classes. In a Hybrid Synchronous format, a student would have 4 separate zoom links and 4 different scheduled cohort times if a student took 4 classes.[CLAIM] Students often find themselves wasting time hunting for zoom links or finding their designated cohort assignments through hundreds of emails or through ELC. The confusing issue of cohorts is further exacerbated if the class rotates it's schedule of designated times for groups of students to come. This indirectly affects an Instructor's ability to teach if students are facing obstacles in joining class online and in-person. The goal of our project is too ease the use
