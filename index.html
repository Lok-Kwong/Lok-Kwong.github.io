Synopsis

In the world of virtual learning, professors and instructors commonly adopt the Hybrid Synchronous Class model in order to engage with students remotely and in-person. With this model comes new challenges and what we sought to tackle was the disorganized nature of Hybrid Synchronous Classes. We seek to find a solution for students that have to scour their email or eLC for Zoom links and scheduled cohort times everyday for multiple classes, as well as to improve an instructor's ability to engage with their students by removing one of their obstacles to learning. The goal of this website is to document our steps in finding and creating a solution that helps students and instructors achieve UGA's mission, "to teach, to serve, and to inquire into the nature of things."
